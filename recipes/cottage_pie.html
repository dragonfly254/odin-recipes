Cottage Pie

Description

This is a short description of the recipe

Ingredients

  • Lean Beef Mince
  • Onions
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Swede
  • frozen Garden Peas
  • Plain Flour
  • Tomatoes
  • Lea and Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • Gravy Granules
  • Water
  • Pearl Barley
  • Washed Salsd Potatoes
  • Sunflower Oil

Getting ready

  1. Peel and dice the onions
  2. Peel and chop the garlic
  3. Peel and dice the carrots
  4. Peel and dice the swede
  5. Make up the gravy granules according to the instructions

Steps

  1. In large pan brown off the mince
  2. To the pan add the onions and garlic, cook for 4-5 minutes
  3. Add the carrots, swede and peas
  4. Stir in the flour
  5. Add the tomatoes, worcestershire sauce, gravy and pearl barley. Season with pepper
  6. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for approximately 20-30 minutes
  7. Cook the potatoes in a pan of boiling water, drain
  8. Using a fork gently crush the potatoes adding the oil and season with pepper
  9. Transfer the prepared mince to an oven proof dish and top with the crushed potatoes
  10. Place in a pre-heated oven at 180C for approximately 15-20 minutes
