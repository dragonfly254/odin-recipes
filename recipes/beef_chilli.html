Beef Chill

Description

This is a short description of the recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Onions
  • Garlic Puree
  • red kidney Beans(in water)
  • Italian Tomato Paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Mild Chilli Powder
  • Red Peppers

Getting ready

  1. Peel and finely chop the onions
  2. Peel and finely chop the red pepper
  3. Drain kidney beans and rinse with cold water

Steps

  1. In a large pan heat vegetable oil on a medium heat then add finely chopped onion and red pepper cook until soft
  2. Add garlic puree and cook for a further 1 minute
  3. Add mince and cook meat for approx. 4-5 minutes before adding tomato puree and cook for further 2 minutes
  4. Add chopped tomato and chilli, turn heat down and cook on a loe heat until chilli thickens
  5. Add red drained kidney beans to chilli and heat through until piping hot
  6. Check seasoning before serving with rice or a jacket potato
