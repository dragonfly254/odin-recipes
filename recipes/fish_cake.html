Fish Cake

Description

This is a short description of the recipe

Ingredients

  • Skinned Hake, Cod or Haddock
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Snipped Chives
  • Peeled Maris Piper Potatoes
  • Finely Chopped Onions
  • Eggs
  • Fresh Breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Dice the peeled potatoes, cover with water, and bring to boil, simmer until cooked
  2. Place the skinned fish into a deep baking tray and cover with the water and milk, place in the the oven and bake for 15 mins or until firm
  3. Strain the potatoes, place back into the pan and dry out over a little heat, put into a bowl, mash and season
  4. Take the cooked fish out of the tray, flake
  5. Add the finely chopped onion, snipped chives and eggs to the potatoes and mix, carefully add the flaked fish to the mixture
  6. Divide the fishcake mixture into 10 or 20 equal amounts, shape into rounds and coat evenly with the breadcrumbs. Place onto the greased oven trays, bake for 15-20 mins turning once.

Serving tip. Serve with peas, a mixed salad or a homemade tomato sauce
